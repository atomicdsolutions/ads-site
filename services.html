Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our IT services, answered clearly and honestly

What makes AtomicD Solutions different from other IT providers?

We offer lightning-fast support (guaranteed 1-hour response for critical issues), certified security and compliance expertise, and a real partnership approach. You always work with someone who knows your business—no bouncing around call centers.

Can you support nonprofits, government contractors, and businesses with specific compliance needs?

Yes! We have deep experience with nonprofits, government contracts, and regulated industries. We help with grant compliance, HIPAA, NIST, and more—plus we tailor our services to fit your unique policies and workflows.

What is managed IT, and do I really need it?

Managed IT means we proactively monitor, maintain, and secure all your tech—so you don't have to wait for problems before getting help. Most organizations save money, reduce downtime, and get more focus by using managed services.

How do you handle mobile device and endpoint management?

We use secure, industry-leading tools to manage all your devices. Whether staff are onsite, remote, or using personal (BYOD) hardware, we keep everything updated, encrypted, and easily supportable—including remote wipe and lock for lost or stolen devices.

How quickly can I get help if something breaks?

For critical issues, we start working within one hour (often faster). Most problems are resolved the same day, and you'll always reach an experienced human, not an automated system.

Can you help us move to the cloud, or improve our remote work setup?

Absolutely! We handle everything from initial planning to migration and staff onboarding for Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, AWS, and more. We'll make your transition seamless, secure, and stress-free.

What if we already have some in-house IT?

We love working collaboratively! We can supplement your current team, take on specialized tasks, or provide backup/help desk coverage—you choose how much or how little help you need.

Is my data safe with AtomicD Solutions?

Yes—security is our top priority. All backups are encrypted, monitored, and tested regularly. We follow best practices for security and compliance, and work with trusted technology partners only.

Do you provide custom service packages?

Yes—every business is different. We'll tailor our offerings to your needs, budget, and team size. Book a free call and we'll build a plan together!
